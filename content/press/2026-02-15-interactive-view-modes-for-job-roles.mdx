Customization is key to productivity. Today we're releasing flexible viewing options for your job roles, giving you control over how you visualize your open positions.

## New Features

The Roles page now features **Interactive View Modes**. You can instantly toggle between **Table**, **Grid**, and **Split** views to suit your preference. Whether you need a data-dense spreadsheet view or a visual card layout, the choice is yours.

We've polished the experience with **Smooth Animations** when switching between these views, making the interface feel responsive and fluid.

## Improvements

Behind the scenes, we've implemented the `RolesMemphisPage` structure to support these diverse layouts without compromising load times or performance.
