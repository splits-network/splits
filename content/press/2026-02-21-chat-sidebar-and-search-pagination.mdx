Efficient communication and navigation are the backbone of a productive workflow. We've introduced a persistent chat sidebar and improved how you browse search results.

## New Features

A new **Chat Sidebar** is now available, incorporating context-aware components. This allows you to keep conversations going while navigating different parts of the platform, ensuring you never miss a beat.

We've added **Search Pagination** and **Result Card Components**. Navigating through large lists of candidates or jobs is now smoother and more intuitive, helping you find what you need faster.

**Onboarding Routes** have been added to the API gateway, streamlining the process for new users, candidates, and recruiters joining the platform.

## Improvements

We've enhanced **Candidate Document Management** and profile settings, giving you more flexibility in managing candidate files. Additionally, job grids now display an **Estimated Payout Range**, providing crucial financial context at a glance.
