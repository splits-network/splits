Today's update focuses on transparency and resource availability. We've made it easier for media and partners to access our brand assets and improved how we communicate system status.

## New Features

We've launched a **Press Kit Page**, providing a central hub for articles, metadata, and brand assets. This makes it simple for press and partners to find accurate information about Splits Network.

Our **Status Page** has been migrated to a dedicated service with an external URL. This ensures that even if our main platform experiences issues, you'll always have access to real-time system status updates.

We've also implemented **Theme Management**, introducing official Dark and Light themes using DaisyUI, so you can choose the interface style that works best for you.

## Improvements

The **Footer Navigation Editor** has been enhanced to handle partial configurations and now includes an import JSON modal, making it easier for admins to update site navigation. We've also enriched our marketing content with new sections on Split Fees, Transparency, and Platform Updates.
