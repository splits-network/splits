Communication is key to a smooth workflow. We've massively expanded our library of email notifications to keep you informed about every important activity on the platform.

## New Features

We've added a wide range of **New Email Templates** to the Notification Service. These include:

- **Monthly Hiring Reports** and **Weekly Activity Digests** to keep you on top of your performance.
- **Team Invitation** emails to help you grow your organization.
- **Billing and Onboarding** notifications to guide users through crucial steps.
- **Tier Promotion/Demotion** alerts to keep you informed about your account status.

## Improvements

The **Portal Navigation** has been updated with full-width menus and better alignment using flexbox layouts. We've also refined the **Cookie Consent** banner and footer layout for a cleaner, more consistent user experience across the site.
