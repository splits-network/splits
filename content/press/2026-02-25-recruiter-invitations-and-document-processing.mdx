Growing your network and system performance are the focus of today's update. We've made it easier to invite collaborators and improved how our system handles documents.

## New Features

We've launched **Recruiter-Company Invitations**. You can now seamlessly invite recruiters to join your company via email, complete with secure link generation and tracking.

We've implemented **Non-blocking Background Document Processing**. This improvement ensures that document processing happens asynchronously when services start up, preventing bottlenecks and keeping the application responsive.

## Improvements

The **Sign-up Flow** has been enhanced with better user data handling and verification steps. We've also updated our OAuth implementation to include OpenID email scopes for Google Calendar, ensuring smoother calendar integrations.
