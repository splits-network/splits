Reliability and design consistency are the themes of today's release. We're aligning our messaging tools with our new design system and giving administrators better visibility into platform health.

## New Features

We've integrated **Basel UI Components** into the portal, updating animations and ensuring a seamless visual experience. Specifically, our messaging system now features **Basel Messaging Components**, bringing them in line with our latest design standards using DaisyUI integration.

For mobile users, we've implemented a **Mobile Detail Overlay**. This ensures that complex grid and split views remain usable and intuitive on smaller screens by presenting details in a clean overlay.

## Under the Hood

We've added **Service Status Debugging Tools**. These tools allow our engineering team to monitor theme management and service health more effectively, ensuring high uptime and quick resolution of any issues.
