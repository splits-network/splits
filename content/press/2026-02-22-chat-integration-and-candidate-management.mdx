We're bringing communication closer to your work. Chat functionality is now integrated directly into your key workflows, and managing candidates has never been easier.

## New Features

**Chat Sidebar Integration** is now live in both Candidate and Recruiter action toolbars. You can initiate and continue conversations right from the context of your work, streamlining your communication process.

We've introduced a robust **Candidate Management Table** complete with sorting and filtering functionality. This gives you a powerful way to organize and view your candidate pipeline.

**Profile Pages** have been enhanced with dynamic section handling and URL synchronization, ensuring that your view state is preserved as you navigate.

## Improvements

We've replaced older menu components with a new **SpeedMenu** across various toolbars, offering a faster and more consistent navigation experience. We've also implemented **Bulk Replace** functionality for job requirements and pre-screen questions, saving you time on repetitive data entry.
