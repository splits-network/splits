Today's release focuses on data richness and security. We're capturing more structured data from resumes to power better matching, and we're encrypting sensitive fields to keep your data safe.

## New Features

The **Application Submission Process** has been enhanced to support **Structured Resume Data**. This means our system can now parse and utilize specific details from resumes more effectively, leading to better candidate matching and screening.

We've implemented **AES-256-GCM Encryption** for sensitive data. This industry-standard encryption ensures that critical information remains secure at rest.

## Improvements

We've enriched applications with **Company Sourcer Data**, giving recruiters better visibility into who sourced a candidate. Additionally, we've implemented a new **Payout Schedule Creation** system for eligible placements, ensuring timely and accurate financial processing.
