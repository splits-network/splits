We're kicking off a series of UI enhancements with a focus on discoverability and design consistency. Today's update brings powerful new search tools and foundational UI components that will streamline your workflow.

## New Features

Recruiters can now leverage an **Advanced Job Search** page. This feature includes comprehensive filtering and sorting capabilities, allowing you to quickly pinpoint the exact roles you're looking for by criteria that matter most to your sourcing strategy.

We've also introduced a collection of **Memphis UI Components** to the design system. These include new Toggle switches, Trust Badges, and tab interfaces (UnderlineTabs and VerticalTabs), ensuring a cohesive and modern look across the platform.

## Under the Hood

To support future development and design showcases, we've added a **ShowcasePage** component. This internal tool helps our team rapidly prototype and test new UI patterns with mock job listings before they go live.
