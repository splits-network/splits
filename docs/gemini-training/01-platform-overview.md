## Platform 1: Splits Network (splits.network)

**Audience:** Recruiters and hiring companies
**Purpose:** A collaborative marketplace designed specifically for recruiter-to-recruiter "split-fee" partnerships
**Pricing:** Freemium — Starter (Free), Pro ($99/mo), Partner ($249/mo); ~16% savings with annual billing

### What It Does

Splits Network is a **split-fee recruiting marketplace** — the only platform built from the ground up for collaborative recruiting rather than bolting split-fee features onto a generic ATS.

Recruiters join the network to access curated job openings, submit candidates, collaborate with other recruiters, and earn transparent commission splits. Companies post roles for free, set their own fee structures, and gain access to a vetted network of specialized recruiters — paying only when a hire is made.

### Key Value Propositions

- **For Recruiters:** "Turn your network into recurring revenue." Access a marketplace of open roles, collaborate on placements, earn 20–40% of placement fees, and track everything in one platform. Average monthly earnings: $8,500+. Time to first placement: 14 days.
- **For Companies:** "A network of recruiters, one simple platform." Post roles for free, access vetted specialized recruiters, get full pipeline visibility, and only pay upon a successful hire. Companies hire 3x faster (avg 14 days vs. 45 days industry average) and reduce recruiting costs by 50%.

### Core Features

1. **Integrated ATS** — Manages jobs, candidates, applications, and placements in one centralized location
2. **5-Role Commission System** — Transparent attribution of up to five roles per placement (Closer, Client, Specs Owner, Discovery, BD) with automated split calculations
3. **AI Candidate-Job Fit Analysis** — AI reviews each application, providing fit scores, strengths, concerns, and skill gap analysis; candidates can revise and resubmit
4. **Multi-Gate Approval Workflows** — Candidate recruiter gate, company recruiter gate, and company decision gate ensure quality at every stage
5. **Real-Time Pipelines** — All parties see where candidates stand in the hiring process at all times
6. **Recruiter-to-Recruiter Collaboration** — Ethical proposals to co-work placements with clear terms
7. **Automated Payout Scheduling** — Stripe-powered payouts with escrow holds during guarantee periods
8. **Gamification** — XP, badges, streaks, and leaderboards to reward active participation
9. **AI-Powered Matching** — Rule-based + vector embedding similarity matching between candidates and jobs
10. **ATS Integrations** — Bidirectional sync with Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, and Ashby

## Platform 2: Applicant Network (applicant.network)

**Audience:** Job seekers and candidates
**Purpose:** A modern candidate-facing portal focused on transparency, communication, and recruiter advocacy
**Pricing:** 100% free for candidates — forever

### What It Does

Applicant Network is the candidate side of the Employment Networks ecosystem. Unlike traditional job boards where applications vanish into black holes, Applicant Network matches candidates with specialized recruiters who advocate for them throughout the hiring process. Candidates get real-time visibility into their application status, expert guidance, and genuine communication.

### Key Value Propositions

- **Expert Recruiter Advocacy:** Get matched with recruiters who specialize in your field and actively champion your candidacy
- **Real-Time Transparency:** See exactly where your application stands at every stage — no more guessing
- **One-Click Apply:** Streamlined application process with resume management
- **Privacy First:** Control what information is shared and when; identity masking until company formally accepts
- **Career Resources:** Industry-specific career guides, salary insights, and interview preparation
- **100% Free:** No fees, no premium tiers, no hidden costs — ever

### How It's Different From LinkedIn or Indeed

"Instead of blasting applications into the void, you get matched with real recruiters who specialize in your field. They advocate for you, prep you for interviews, and make sure you never get ghosted."

Recruiters on the platform are measured by responsiveness (95% response rate within 48 hours), creating accountability that traditional job boards lack.

## The Connected Ecosystem

Employment Networks creates a single ecosystem where all three parties — recruiters, companies, and candidates — interact transparently:

```
Companies post roles on Splits Network
         ↓
Recruiters browse roles, source & submit candidates
         ↓
Candidates apply via Applicant Network or are submitted by recruiters
         ↓
Multi-gate approval process with full visibility for all parties
         ↓
Successful hire → Automated commission splits → Everyone wins
```

### Why "Two Platforms, One Ecosystem"

The two platforms share a single backend infrastructure and database but present purpose-built experiences for their respective audiences:

- **Splits Network** is optimized for the recruiter and company workflow: posting jobs, managing pipelines, tracking commissions, collaborating on placements
- **Applicant Network** is optimized for the candidate experience: discovering opportunities, applying, tracking status, communicating with recruiters

This separation ensures each audience gets a focused, uncluttered experience while maintaining seamless data flow between all parties.

## The Industry Problem Employment Networks Solves

### Recruiting is Broken for Everyone

| Stakeholder | Pain Point |
|-------------|-----------|
| **Recruiters** | Spreadsheet chaos for split-fee tracking; fragmented tools that don't talk to each other; unclear fee structures; no reliable way to find collaboration partners |
| **Candidates** | Applications vanish into black holes; ghosted after interviews with no feedback; no visibility into where they stand; no advocate in the process |
| **Companies** | Managing dozens of recruiter contracts; no visibility into recruiter activity; inconsistent fee structures; no centralized pipeline view |

### How Employment Networks Fixes It

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Transparency** | All fee structures, commission splits, and pipeline stages are visible to relevant parties in real time |
| **Fairness** | The 5-role commission system ensures everyone who contributes to a placement is fairly compensated |
| **Simplicity** | One platform replaces spreadsheets, email chains, and fragmented tools |
| **Accountability** | Response rate tracking, gate approvals, and AI-powered quality checks maintain standards |
| **Modern Technology** | AI matching, vector search, real-time WebSocket updates, and automated financial workflows |
