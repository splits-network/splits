| Feature | Starter (Free) | Pro ($99/mo) | Partner ($249/mo) |
|---------|----------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Access to roles | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Candidate submissions | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| ATS pipeline | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Commission payout bonus | Base rates | Enhanced rates | Premium rates |
| Annual billing discount | — | 20% savings | 20% savings |

- $0 hidden fees — the monthly subscription is the only recurring cost
- No penalties for quiet months
- Switch plans anytime
- Free trial available for paid plans

### Company Pricing

- **Free to post roles** — no listing fees, no subscription required
- **Pay only on successful hire** — fee as a percentage of first-year salary
- **Company sets the fee percentage** — full control
- **Guarantee period** — default 90 days, customizable
- **Net terms available** — immediate, net-30, net-60, net-90

### Candidate Pricing

- **100% free — forever.** No premium tiers, no upsells, no hidden costs.

| Aspect | Traditional Firm | Splits Network |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| Fee transparency | Opaque, negotiated per deal | Visible to all parties upfront |
| Collaboration | Rare, informal arrangements | Built-in collaboration with clear split terms |
| Candidate visibility | Candidates rarely know their status | Real-time pipeline tracking |
| Technology | Email + spreadsheets | AI matching, automated payouts, real-time chat |
| Geographic reach | Limited to firm's network | 2,000+ recruiters across specializations |

## Trust and Security

- **Clerk Authentication** — Enterprise-grade authentication and identity management
- **Identity Verification** — Stripe Connect identity verification for recruiter payouts
- **Document Scanning** — ClamAV antivirus scanning for all uploaded documents
- **TLS Encryption** — All traffic encrypted with TLS 1.2/1.3
- **Privacy Controls** — Candidates control what information is shared; identity masking until formal acceptance
- **Audit Trails** — Complete logging of all stage transitions, financial transactions, and relationship changes
- **Guarantee Period Protection** — Escrow holds protect against failed placements
