Comprehensive Splits Network Evaluation: Missing Features & Gaps
Based on my thorough evaluation of the platform from both recruiter and company perspectives, here's what I found:
RECRUITER PERSPECTIVE - What's Missing
Job & Role Management

No job posting templates - Recruiters must create each job from scratch
Limited role customization - Few options for specifying unique requirements or deal structures
No bulk role management - Can't manage multiple roles with similar settings efficiently
Missing role expiration/renewal workflow - No automated reminders or re-activation system
No role analytics - Can't track which roles get most views/applications over time
Missing role sharing metrics - No data on how many times a role was shared or by whom

Candidate Management

No candidate sourcing tools - No boolean search, filters by skills, location matching
Missing bulk candidate import - Can't upload candidates via CSV/spreadsheet
No candidate pipeline templates - Each candidate workflow is manual
Limited candidate matching - No AI-powered matching to suggest candidates for roles
No candidate communication templates - Missing email/message templates for outreach
Missing candidate rating/tagging system - Can't organize candidates with custom tags or rankings
No bulk messaging - Can't send messages to multiple candidates at once
Missing candidate historical data - No past placements or success history tracking

Application & Pipeline Management

Very limited pipeline stages - Only basic stages shown; no customization
No pipeline automation - Can't set automatic stage progression or triggers
Missing application templates/forms - No custom intake forms for specific roles
No rejection reasons tracking - Weak feedback loop on why candidates weren't selected
Missing interview scheduling integration - No calendar sync or automated scheduling
No offer management workflow - Can't create/track competing offers or counteroffers
Missing onboarding tracking - No visibility into candidate start date or first-day confirmation

Communication & Collaboration

Limited messaging features - Basic messaging but no scheduling, read receipts, or status
No in-app notifications settings - Can't customize notification preferences
Missing bulk communication - No broadcast or template-based messaging campaigns
No communication history - Can't search or archive past conversations
Missing team chat/collaboration - No internal team communication within the platform
No communication templates - Recruiters must write custom messages each time
Missing handoff features - Difficult to transition candidates between team members

Earnings & Finance

No detailed commission breakdown - Limited visibility into how fees are calculated
Missing commission projections - Can't forecast earnings based on pipeline
No payment history/invoicing - Limited tracking of past payouts
Missing payment schedule visibility - Unclear payment terms per company/role
No tax document generation - No 1099 or tax-related reporting
Missing fee comparison tools - Can't easily compare rates across roles/companies

Analytics & Reporting

Very basic dashboard metrics - Limited KPI visibility
No conversion funnel tracking - Can't see drop-off rates by stage
Missing success rate analytics - No detailed placement success metrics
No performance benchmarking - Can't compare against other recruiters
Missing role-specific analytics - No data on which role types perform best
No candidate source tracking - Unclear which sourcing methods work best
Missing time-to-hire metrics - No tracking of average hire cycle times

Search & Discovery

Limited role search filters - Basic filters but no advanced criteria
No role recommendations - Platform doesn't suggest roles based on recruiter specialty
Missing saved searches - Can't save role search filters for future use
No role alerts - Recruiters aren't notified of new matching roles

Professional Profile & Marketplace

Profile strength score is confusing - 64% complete but unclear what increases it
Missing professional certifications section - No way to showcase credentials
No placement track record display - Success metrics aren't prominently featured
Limited portfolio/case studies - Can't showcase past successful placements
Missing calendar/availability - No scheduling system for candidate meetings
No testimonials/reviews section - Can't collect feedback from candidates or companies

Integrations & Workflows

No ATS integrations - Can't sync with existing systems (LinkedIn, Greenhouse, etc.)
Missing email integration - No Gmail/Outlook sync
No CRM integration - Can't sync with Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.
Missing Slack/Teams integration - No notifications to external communication tools
No calendar integration - Can't sync interviews/calls to Google/Outlook calendar
Missing data export - Limited ability to export custom reports

PRIORITY RECOMMENDATIONS FOR MAXIMUM IMPACT
For Recruiters (Highest Impact First)

AI-powered candidate matching to suggest candidates for roles
Bulk candidate import (CSV upload)
Commission projections & earnings forecasting
Advanced role search & saved searches
Automated pipeline workflow with triggers
Email templates & bulk messaging
ATS integrations (LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Workday)
Detailed pipeline analytics (conversion rates, time-to-hire)

For Companies (Highest Impact First)

Recruiter performance tracking & analytics
Collaborative review tools for candidate evaluation
Offer management workflow with templates
Advanced recruiter vetting & verification
Role performance analytics (fill time, quality)
Batch candidate actions (move multiple through pipeline)
Payment/fee dispute resolution system
Slack/Teams integration for notifications

For Candidates (Highest Impact First)

Job recommendations based on profile/preferences
Real-time application tracking with detailed status
Interview scheduling directly in platform
One-click apply (smart form pre-fill)
Job alerts for saved searches
Interview feedback from companies
Offer comparison tools
Mobile-responsive candidate experience

This platform has strong core infrastructure for split-fee recruiting, but lacks the depth of features in candidate matching, communications automation, analytics, and integrations that would make it truly competitive with established ATS platforms.
