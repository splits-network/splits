**30 seconds:**
"Splits Network is a transparent, AI-powered recruiting marketplace that enables recruiters to collaborate fairly, companies to hire with control, and candidates to get intelligent feedback. Built on modern microservices architecture for scale and reliability."

**60 seconds:**

Recruiters can propose collaboration opportunities without poaching represented candidates. Companies get multi-level approval workflows and visibility. Candidates receive AI-powered feedback and can revise applications. And our automated payout system eliminates manual administration. It's recruiting technology for the modern era."
