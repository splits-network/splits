## Competitive Matrix

### vs. Traditional Recruiting Firms

- **Fees**: Transparent vs. Opaque (20-40%)
- **Speed**: 1-2 weeks vs. 2-4 weeks
- **Network**: Unlimited marketplace vs. Limited to firm staff
- **Technology**: Cloud-native vs. Legacy systems
- **Recruiter Access**: Independent + teams vs. Only employees

**Key Message**: "Professional recruiting, modern economics"

### vs. Job Boards (LinkedIn, Indeed)

- **Target**: Recruiters & Companies vs. Job seekers
- **Tools**: Purpose-built vs. Minimal recruiter tools
- **Commission Management**: Automated vs. None
- **Collaboration**: Built-in vs. None
- **Quality Gating**: Multi-gate vs. None

**Key Message**: "Built for recruiters, not generalists"

### vs. Recruiting Platforms (Upwork, Guru)

- **Specialization**: Recruiting-focused vs. Generalist
- **Commission Model**: Transparent 5-role vs. Opaque variable
- **Financial Automation**: Escrow + payouts vs. Limited
- **Community**: Collaborative vs. Transactional
- **Enterprise Features**: Full workflows vs. Minimal

**Key Message**: "Finally, a platform built specifically for recruiting"

"We're built specifically for recruiting with three things competitors don't have: transparent 5-role commissions, ethical recruiter collaboration system, and AI with candidate revision. Plus, we handle all the payout automation."

**"How do you prevent commission disputes?"**
"Complete audit trails. Every decision logged, every rate visible, every payout tracked. Transparency prevents disputes from starting."

"We attract quality recruiters through transparent economics and fair treatment. Plus, companies maintain quality control through gate approvals."
