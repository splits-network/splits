**expire-recording-cleanup.ts** (~150 lines):
Follow the exact pattern of `send-weekly-digest.ts` (shebang, env var validation, logger setup, main function with try/catch, process.exit).

The job does two things:

1. **Send 2-day warning emails** for Free tier recordings that will expire in 2 days:
   - Query: Find call_recordings where:
     - `created_at` is between 5 and 5.5 days ago (i.e., will be deleted in ~2 days)
     - The call creator's current subscription tier is 'starter' (join calls → users → subscriptions → plans)
     - No warning email was already sent (check `notification_log` for a `recording_expiry_warning` event with matching `call_id`)
   - For each recording: send email via Resend to the creator's email address with subject "Your call recording will be deleted in 2 days"
   - Log to `notification_log` to prevent duplicate warnings
   - Email body should include: call title (or "Untitled call"), recording date, and a CTA button "Upgrade to keep your recordings" linking to the portal subscription page

2. **Delete expired Free tier recordings** older than 7 days:
   - Query: Find call_recordings where:
     - `created_at` is older than 7 days
     - The call creator's current subscription tier is 'starter'
   - For each expired recording:
     - Delete the blob from Supabase storage (`call-recordings` bucket) using the `blob_url` field
     - Delete the call_recordings row from the database
   - Log the count of deleted recordings

Pro tier retention: 90 days. Partner tier: unlimited (no cleanup). The job should also clean up Pro recordings older than 90 days following the same pattern (delete blob + row, no warning email needed since Pro users had transcription).

Use service-role Supabase client for all operations. The tier check must be real-time (query current subscription, not cached).

**expire-recording-cleanup.yaml** (~40 lines):
Follow the exact pattern of existing CronJob YAMLs in `infra/k8s/notification-service/cronjobs/`. Key settings:
- `schedule: "0 3 * * *"` (daily at 3 AM UTC)
- `concurrencyPolicy: Forbid`
- Container runs: `node dist/jobs/expire-recording-cleanup.js`
- Same env vars as other notification-service jobs (SUPABASE_URL, SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY, RESEND_API_KEY, FROM_EMAIL, NEXT_PUBLIC_PORTAL_URL)
- `restartPolicy: OnFailure`
- `backoffLimit: 3`
