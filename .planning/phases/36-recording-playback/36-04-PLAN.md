  - Heading: "This Interview Will Be Recorded" (`text-lg font-black`)

- Keep under 60 lines

**recording-indicator.tsx** -- New component:
- Props: `{ isRecording: boolean; onStopRecording?: () => void; canStop: boolean }`
- When `isRecording` is true:
  - Shows a persistent bar/badge in the controls area: red pulsing dot (CSS animation `animate-pulse`) + "Recording" text in `text-sm font-semibold text-error`
  - If `canStop` is true (interviewer), shows a stop button: `btn btn-ghost btn-sm text-error` with `fa-duotone fa-regular fa-stop` icon
- When not recording: renders nothing
- The pulsing dot: `<span className="inline-block w-2.5 h-2.5 bg-error rounded-full animate-pulse" />`
- Keep under 50 lines

**use-recording-state.ts** -- New hook:
- `useRecordingState(interviewId: string, apiBase: string, getToken: () => Promise<string | null>, isInterviewer: boolean)`
- State: `isRecording`, `isProcessing`, `recordingStatus`
- `startRecording()`: POST to `/api/v2/interviews/{id}/recording/start`, update state
- `stopRecording()`: POST to `/api/v2/interviews/{id}/recording/stop`, update state
- `submitConsent(token?: string)`: POST to `/api/v2/interviews/{id}/recording/consent`
- Polls recording status every 5 seconds via GET `/api/v2/interviews/{id}/recording` to detect when recording starts (for candidates who join after recording begins)
- Returns `{ isRecording, isProcessing, recordingStatus, startRecording, stopRecording, submitConsent }`
- Keep under 80 lines

**index.ts** -- Add exports for new components and hook:
- Export `RecordingConsent`, `RecordingIndicator`, `useRecordingState`
