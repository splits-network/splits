    - `replicas: ${REPLICAS:-2}`

    - Container `video`, image `${ACR_SERVER}/video:${IMAGE_TAG}`, port 3102

      - `PORT`: `3102`

    - Liveness probe: GET / on port 3102, initialDelay 30s, period 10s
    - Readiness probe: GET / on port 3102, initialDelay 10s, period 5s

    Service:
    - `name: video`, `namespace: splits-network`
    - `type: ClusterIP`
    - Port 80 -> targetPort 3102

             - path: /
               pathType: Prefix
               backend:
                 service:
                   name: video
                   port:
                     number: 80
